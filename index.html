Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Small tasks can be completed in 1-5 hours, while larger projects may take 1-2 weeks. I always provide an estimated timeline before starting work and keep you updated on progress.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payments through PayPal, Robux, and other digital payment methods. For larger projects, I'm open to discussing milestone-based payment plans to ensure your comfort and security.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! I provide 7 days of free support for bug fixes and minor adjustments after project completion. For ongoing updates and major changes, we can discuss a maintenance agreement.

Can you work with existing codebases?

Absolutely! I have experience working with existing codebases and can seamlessly integrate new features or fix issues in your current project. I'll always maintain your coding style and structure.

What if I need changes after the project is completed?

I offer reasonable revision periods for each project. Minor adjustments are typically included, while major changes may require additional discussion. I'm always flexible and want to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product.
